Privacy Policy

About Us

This privacy policy applies to the Fancy Jewellers App developed by Mukul Rai ("we," "us," or "our"), available on the Google Play Store. This policy explains how we handle data when you use our application to check live gold rates and calculate jewelry rates.

Data Collection and Use

The Fancy Jewellers App is designed with your privacy in mind. Here's what you should know about how we handle data:

  • Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from users.
  • App Functionality Data: All calculations and operations are performed locally on your device.
  • Device Permissions: Our app only requests essential permissions required for basic functionality, including SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW for displaying overlay windows when necessary.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, even though we don't collect personal data. All calculations are performed locally on your device, ensuring complete privacy. Our app uses secure HTTPS connections only, as we have disabled cleartext traffic for enhanced security.

Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with third-party services to fetch live gold rates. These services include:

  • Gold Rate API providers
  • Google Play Services

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies for more information.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since we don't collect or store any user data on our servers, there is no data retention period. All local data can be removed by uninstalling the app from your device.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed for general audience use.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
